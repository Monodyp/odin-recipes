Menudo

The Classic Ulam. Loved by many filipinos, all across the country, it is a standard dish, and it is here to Stay. The staple viand, with sharp flavors that pierce through the taste buds, The Dark Dish will always be a classic

Ingredients

Below are the ingredients you need to cook the Classic Adobo

  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • 1 kilo Chicken, butchered
  • 3 medium sized Onions, diced
  • 6 cloves of Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • 3 bay leaves

Procedures

Below are the following steps to take the ingredients above, and perfectly concoct the Dark Dish, the Adobo

  1. Marinate the chicken in soy sauce and garlic.
  2. Pan-fry the chicken.
  3. Pour marinade into the pot and add water.
  4. Simmer for 20 to 25 minutes
  5. Season and serve
